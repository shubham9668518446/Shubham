01 — Profile

About Me

I am a PhD scholar with a strong foundation in botanical and ecological research, particularly in the field of lichenology, phycology, and molecular taxonomy. My work bridges fieldwork and laboratory-based investigations to unravel the biodiversity and biopotential of cryptogamic organisms.

My expertise includes conducting comprehensive field surveys in diverse botanical settings — parks, forests, and biosphere reserves — using GPS mapping, lichen monitoring, and herbarium preparation and documentation.

In the laboratory, I specialize in phytochemical screening, molecular characterization, phylogenetic analysis, and bioactivity studies of algae, photobionts, and lichens. My research has yielded publications in reputed journals including Elsevier, Springer, and Begell House.
